Dear NYSOMA State Tournament Official or Volunteer,
In order to comply with new tournament requirements from Binghamton University, NYSOMA was required to issue a child protection policy and to provide this policy to all officials and volunteers participating in the tournament. A copy of the new policy can be found here. As part of this policy there is also an acknowledgement form that you will be required to sign and submit in order to complete your tournament registration and receive your identification badge. Click here to download the policy. It would be helpful if you would complete this form at home and bring it with you to the officials' registration on Friday, April 1, at the DoubleTree Hotel in Binghamton. Registration will be open from 3:00PM until 7:00PM prior to the general meeting. Volunteers not attending the general meeting can turn in this form the day of the tournament to receive their identification badges.
Thank you for your cooperation and I will see you this weekend.
John Fallon
NYSOMA Judges' Coordinator
