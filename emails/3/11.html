2014-15 Grant Awards Announced!

 

Board President Elizabeth Walker states: "The Board of Directors is very pleased to award 20 grants totaling $500,000 to serve young, low-income expectant and new parents throughout New York State.  Services are located in 23 counties across New York State in high need areas where services are often very limited. We are very grateful to Governor Cuomo and the New York State Legislature for their continued support for these very critical services." 

 
The 2014 Grantees are:
Catholic Charities of Buffalo
YWCA of Jamestown
Cornell Cooperative Extension of Allegany and Cattaraugus
Mercy Community Services
Catholic Charities of Livingston

Oswego Opportunities
